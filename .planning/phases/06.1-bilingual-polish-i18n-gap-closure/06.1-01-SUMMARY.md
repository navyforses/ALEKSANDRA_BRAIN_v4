## Decisions Made

- **summarize / relativeTime accept `t` as an argument, not inlined.** The plan permitted either approach. Passing `t` keeps the helpers pure (no module-level hook calls, no React-internals coupling), preserves the Phase 5 test-friendly shape, and the only call site is a single render branch in ActivityFeed.tsx — there is no maintenance gain from inlining.
- **Nested JSON shape (Manager.summarize.\*, etc.) over flat key names.** Improves readability of the dictionaries; the inline missing-key verifier expands `summarize.undidAction` etc. via `key.split('.').reduce(...)` so resolution cost is identical.
- **Translated dormant InputBar / VoiceRecorder anyway.** Plan D-02 explicitly authorized this ("cheap and may be re-mounted"); skipping them would force a follow-up plan when they re-mount.
- **Manager namespace placed alphabetically between Knowledge and Navigation.** Minimizes merge surface for 06.1-02 (depends_on=[06.1-01]) which appends 4 ENUM namespaces (`TimelineEventType`, `HypothesisStatus`, `HypothesisType`, `ConfidenceLevel`) — those will land further down with zero overlap.
