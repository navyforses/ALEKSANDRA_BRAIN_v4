## Concept A Prompt — Clinical Intelligence Command Center

Create a desktop web app UI mockup for a pediatric HIE living research brain platform. Subject: ALEKSANDRA_BRAIN clinical intelligence command center showing evidence registry, validated hypotheses, therapy candidates, clinical timeline, and a right-side AI research assistant. Composition: 16:9 desktop browser frame, dark navy medical interface, left vertical navigation, central dashboard with cards and charts, top hero panel titled “ALEKSANDRA_BRAIN”, a large evidence-to-hypothesis pipeline visualization, and a persistent right panel named “BRAIN assistant”. Style: high-end medical SaaS dashboard, dark mode, cyan and violet neural glow accents, clean Georgian/English mixed UI labels, precise card hierarchy, realistic spacing. Text/content to render: “Living Research Brain”, “Evidence Map”, “Hypothesis Validation”, “Therapy Candidates”, “Timeline”, “Doctor Mode”, “Family-safe summary”, “Not medical advice”. Constraints: visual mockup only, no real patient data, no clutter, avoid unreadable tiny text, avoid scary hospital imagery.

## Concept B Prompt — Family-Safe Research Journey

Create a desktop web app UI mockup for families following a pediatric HIE research and care-support workflow. Subject: ALEKSANDRA_BRAIN family-safe dashboard explaining what the system knows today, what changed, what evidence is being reviewed, and what questions to ask doctors. Composition: 16:9 desktop browser frame, warm light interface, large reassuring hero message, progress timeline, simple cards for “Today”, “Evidence under review”, “Questions for doctor”, “Therapy watchlist”, and a calm right-side “Ask the Brain” assistant. Style: premium healthcare family portal, soft white background, pale blue and mint palette, rounded cards, humane typography, gentle illustrations of connected knowledge nodes. Text/content to render: “Family View”, “What changed today”, “Evidence being reviewed”, “Ask your doctor”, “Safety boundary”, “Research support — doctors decide treatment”. Constraints: friendly but serious, avoid childish visuals, avoid diagnosis claims, avoid complex medical jargon.

## Concept C Prompt — Digital Twin Brain Lab

Create a desktop web app UI mockup for a pediatric HIE digital twin brain lab. Subject: ALEKSANDRA_BRAIN 3D brain viewer with MRI upload state, lesion/preserved tissue layers, role-based tabs for doctor, parent, and researcher, and linked evidence panels. Composition: 16:9 desktop browser frame, central translucent 3D brain visualization on dark gradient canvas, left layer controls, bottom timeline scrubber, right evidence and hypothesis inspector, top role tabs. Style: futuristic but credible medical research interface, deep navy background, glassmorphism panels, cyan/magenta highlights, subtle volumetric 3D brain rendering, professional clinical design. Text/content to render: “Digital Twin”, “MRI layers”, “Doctor view”, “Parent view”, “Researcher view”, “HIE affected regions”, “Evidence links”, “Upload MRI”. Constraints: no gore, no photoreal patient body, no claim of diagnosis, keep UI readable and clinically trustworthy.
